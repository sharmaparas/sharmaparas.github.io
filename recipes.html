Recipies

Samosa
The pastry is made from all purpose flour and with few tablespoons vegetable oil. Make a cone and for filling use mashed potatoes, finely chopped onions, green chilies, cilantro and grated ginger mixed with salt, red chilli powder, garam masala. Close the cone and deep fry untill golden brown.



Paneer Pakora
Take indian cottage cheese cut into slices. For filling you can use mashed potatoes, finely chopped onions, green chilies, cilantro and grated ginger mixed with salt, red chilli powder, garam masala. Take chickpea flour and mix with water to for even batter. Take 2 triangular slices fill the filling between and dip in batter and deep fry.



Chicken Tikka
Take chicken thigh fillets and curt into 2-2.5 inch pieces. Take curd, edible red colour, salt, red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander power, turmeric powder, garlic powder, freshly chopped ginger and mix well. Marinate the chicken pieces overnight. In oven at 200 degree celcius cook for 25-30 min with few teaspoon of oil sprinkled over the last few minutes.



Vegetable Kebab
Take finely chopped carrot, peas, mashed potatoes, cilantro, coriander, green beans mixed with salt, red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander power, turmeric powder, garlic powder, freshly chopped ginger and mix well. In the oven at 150 degree celcius cook for 20-25 min until golden brown.



Dal Makhni
Take 3/4th cup urad beans and 1/4th cup kidney beans. Soak them in water overnight(8-9 hours). Take 2 finely chopped onions in a, 2 tomatoes pureed, dry spices like cardamoms, cumin seeds, 2-3 cloves, Bay leaf. In a pan put 3 tablespoon of butter and add dry spicies, then add onions until they turn brown followed by tomato puree. Add salt, red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander power, turmeric powder, garlic powder, freshly chopped ginger. In the mean while take the beans and pressure cook them for 15-20 mins untill the beans are soft. Add the cooked lentils with the sauce. Boil for 10-15 mins. Then add cream and garnish with fresh onions and coriander.



Butter Chicken
Take 3/4th cup yogurt, wisk it and add lime juice, turmeric powder, red chillie, garam masala, cumin powder and mix 2-2.5 inch chicken thigh fillets and marinate for 3 hours. Take 1/4th cup cashew nuts and soak in water. Grill the chicken on a pan. In a sauce pan put 2 table spoon oil, add chopped onions, freshly chopped garlic(2 cloves) and fresh ginger(1 ginger finely chopped as well). Add tomatoes and salt and red chilli powder, cumin powder, garam masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder and cook untill oil starts to seperate from tomatoes. Blend cashew nuts by adding 1/4th cup water and add to sauce. Cook until the mixture is homogeneous and then add grilled chicken pieces. Cook for 10-15 mins and garnish with freshly chopped coriander.



Malai Kofta
For dumplings take Indian grated paneer(cottage cheese) and mashed potatoes, 1/4th teaspoon red chilli powder, 1/4th tablespoon cornflour, pinch of garam masala, pich of salt and milk powder. Mix the ingredients well and deep fry to make dumplings. In a pan ad 2 tablespoon oil followed by 1/2 cup chopped onions utill they turn brown, add ginger garlic paste and again cook for 3-5 mins. Add 1/2 cup tomato puree. Add salt to taste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cardamom powder, followed by cashew paste(1/4 cup) and add little bit of garam masala. Cook for another 10 mins on medium heat. Add the dumplings into the sauce and cook for 3-5 mins. They are ready to be served.



Karahi Paneer
Take 2-3 inch big cubes of cottage cheese. In a pan add 2 table spoon of oil followed by 2 chopped onions and cook untill brown. Add 2 chopped tomatoes and 3 finely chopped green chillies followed by salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala. Cook untill the oil and tomatoes start to seperate. Add 2 chooped capsicum and cook for 2-3 mins and then add paneer. On medium heat cook for another 10-15 mins. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve.
